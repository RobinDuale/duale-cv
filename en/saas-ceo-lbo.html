SaaS CEO under LBO.
Leading B2B SaaS transformation in a PE environment.

Robin Dualé works as CEO / Managing Director for B2B SaaS, Data and information services companies from €10M to €100M in revenue, in transformation, build-up, LBO, exit preparation and strategic AI repositioning contexts.

When do you need a SaaS CEO under LBO?

A SaaS company under LBO does not only need a leader who can articulate a vision. It needs an operator capable of turning that vision into indicators, organization, execution rhythm and measurable value creation.

  • Transforming a legacy model toward subscription or SaaS
  • Commercial, product or operational restructuring post-acquisition
  • Churn reduction, improved recurring revenue and ARR / NRR management
  • Preparing a new equity story for a private equity fund
  • Build-up, acquisition integration and value creation under PE constraints
  • Revising product and go-to-market strategy around AI

Relevant experience

At WEKA France, Robin Dualé led a portfolio of 7 companies, €50M in revenue and 300 employees, with 5 acquisitions integrated, transformation toward digital subscription, co-investment in the Paragon Partners LBO and 6 exit processes completed.

At Quick-FDS / 3E Group / New Mountain Capital, he managed the post-acquisition integration of a B2B regulatory platform, migrated 100% of the customer base to a subscription model and kept churn below 5% throughout the transition.

The profile PE funds look for

The right SaaS CEO under LBO is not necessarily a high-profile founder. It is an operator capable of managing the P&L, structuring the organization, securing the customer base, arbitrating AI investments, working with a PE board and building a credible trajectory toward exit.
